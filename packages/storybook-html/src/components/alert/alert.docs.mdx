## Overview

The alert components are used to notify users of important information or changes on a page attracting the user's attention without interrupting the user's task. The alerts are mainly categorised as page alerts and global alerts.

## Page alerts (Default example)

Page alerts are light versions of alerts that are used locally on pages along with components or on its own to notify users of important information or changes on a page.

## Global alerts (Variant examples)

Global alerts are darker variants of alerts that are used to convey a globally important information that appears at the top of the .

### Info global

<Canvas withSource="none">
  <Story id="components-alert--info-global" />
</Canvas>

```html
<div class="uq-alert uq-alert--info uq-alert--dark" role="alert">
  <div class="uq-alert__container">
    <div class="uq-alert__message">
      <p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.</p>
      <a href="#" title="Link" class="uq-button--inline">Link</a>
    </div>
    <a href="javascript:void(0)" class="uq-alert__close">Close</a>
  </div>
</div>
```
